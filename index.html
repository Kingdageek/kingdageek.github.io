About Me

Nonso is a Software Engineer with over four (4) years of professional experience and about seven (7) years of programming experience. He works primarily with Javascript/ReactJs/NodeJs, PHP/Laravel, and Java/Springboot. You can see his Github projects here: https://github.com/Kingdageek. He is also interested in data science and has completed a 12-week MIT IDSS Data Science course, see his e-portfolio here: https://eportfolio.mygreatlearning.com/chinonso-kingsley-mgbechi. He graduated with a first-class degree in Physics from the Michael Okpara University of Agriculture, Umudike in 2017. He received the NNPC/TEPNG undergraduate scholarship award in 2014. In 2016, He was among the top 10% of about 3000 people selected to attend the Platform's Young Professionals Bootcamp. In October 2017, he led his team to win the WAAW Foundation's award of honour for building the best project at the S(he) Hacks Africa Cohort #3 Bootcamp - a website that matches volunteers to NGOs. Currently, he is pursuing a Master's degree program in Materials Science and Engineering at Kiel University with a scholarship from the Petroleum Technology Development Fund (PTDF), Nigeria, and the Deutscher Akademischer Austauschdienst (DAAD). He is really passionate about computer programming and renewable energy. He also enjoys playing Chess, learning German, and visiting new places. To get in touch, send an email to mgbechinonso@gmail.com.
